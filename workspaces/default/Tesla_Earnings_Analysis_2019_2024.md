# Tesla Q3 Earnings Trend Analysis (2019-2024)

## Overview
This report analyzes Tesla's (TSLA) Q3 performance over the last five years, tracking revenue growth, profitability, and EPS. 

## Data Table (Q3 Figures)

| Quarter | Revenue ($B) | Net Income ($B) | EPS (Adj) |
|---------|--------------|-----------------|-----------|
| 2019 Q3 | 6.30         | 0.143           | 0.18      |
| 2020 Q3 | 8.77         | 0.300           | 0.27      |
| 2021 Q3 | 13.76        | 1.618           | 0.62      |
| 2022 Q3 | 21.45        | 3.292           | 1.05      |
| 2023 Q3 | 23.35        | 1.853           | 0.53      |
| 2024 Q3 | 25.18        | 2.173           | 0.72      |

## Key Findings
- **Revenue Growth:** Tesla has shown consistent year-over-year revenue growth in Q3, though the growth rate has decelerated significantly from the peak expansion seen in 2021-2022.
- **Profitability Challenges:** After hitting a peak EPS of $1.05 in Q3 2022, margins faced pressure in 2023 due to price cuts and aggressive competition. Q3 2024 shows a recovery in EPS to $0.72.
- **2024 Q3 Context:** Tesla reported Q3 2024 results on October 23, 2024, beating analyst expectations for EPS ($0.72 vs $0.58 estimated) while revenue ($25.18B) came in slightly below expectations. Growth was supported by FSD software contributions and operational efficiencies.
